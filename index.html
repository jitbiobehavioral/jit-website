The Aftercare Gap

Why current digital health tools fail in the moments that matter most.

Passive Logging is Too Late

Current digital health tools rely on passive journaling and cloud-dependent processing. By the time a clinician reviews a "high-risk" flag in a web dashboard, the critical 15-minute window for intervention has already closed.

Privacy Compromises Honesty

Patients hesitate to honestly record cravings when they know the data is uploaded to corporate servers and monitored by staff. Without absolute, guaranteed privacy, the intervention tool is rendered useless.

Active, Localized Shielding

The JIT Cortex-Zero Protocol operates entirely on the user's device, providing instantaneous intervention without ever compromising privacy.

100% Zero-Cloud

All processing happens locally on the device. No journal entry or location data ever leaves the user's phone. This guarantees absolute honesty and achieves HIPAA-level privacy by default.

The Vanguard System

A physical shield. Local geofencing actively monitors proximity to defined high-risk areas. If a breach is detected, it silently and instantly triggers an SMS alert to a designated support contact.

The Jolt System

A cognitive shield. Local NLP detects crisis language and physically locks the app, forcing a 90-second working-memory challenge with haptics to interrupt amygdala processing and prevent relapse.
