Closing the Outpatient Blind Spot.

Cortex-Zero is a zero-cloud digital shield that autonomously intercepts behavioral crises and generates objective telemetry for Intensive Outpatient Programs.

Our Mission & Core Beliefs

"To engineer clinical-grade digital shields that protect outpatients in the blind spots of recovery, driven by lived experience and a relentless commitment to data sovereignty."

1. Lived Experience Drives Engineering

We do not build from textbooks. Cortex-Zero was engineered by understanding the exact mechanics of relapse and the coded manipulation that traditional apps miss.

2. Absolute Data Sovereignty

Privacy is a clinical requirement. Our Zero-Cloud architecture ensures behavioral telemetry never leaves the physical device, protecting both the patient and the clinic from third-party data liabilities.

3. Objective Telemetry

Subjective memory fails during a crisis. We believe clinicians deserve autonomous, real-time data to intercept high-risk behavior before a relapse occurs.

Our Commitment: The 10% Profit Pledge

We are stakeholders in patient success. JIT Biobehavioral is legally structured with an Irrevocable Profit Allocation Mandate in our corporate bylaws. We pledge a minimum of 10% of our net annual profit directly back into the addiction recovery ecosystem as grants, software subsidies, and direct funding to publicly funded Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOPs).

Enterprise-Grade Architecture

Designed strictly for clinical deployment. We track behavior, not just vocabulary.

The Threat Cache

Cortex-Zero autonomously normalizes and tags incoming texts from high-risk burner phones, silencing them before they hijack patient attention. It operates entirely in the background.

Zero-Tolerance Guardian

If a patient attempts to reply to a flagged threat, the Guardian service instantly locks the screen and forces a clinical intervention. We neutralize coded manipulation the exact millisecond it happens.

Vanguard Telemetry

Stop relying on subjective memory. Generate objective GPS heatmaps and behavioral risk logs for clinical review. All telemetry is processed directly on the device.

Zero-Cloud Privacy

Patient health data and behavioral logs never leave the physical phone. By eliminating cloud servers entirely, we remove third-party data liability for your clinic.
