Closing the Outpatient Blind Spot.

Cortex-Zero is a zero-cloud digital shield that autonomously intercepts behavioral crises and generates objective telemetry for Intensive Outpatient Programs.

Our Mission

"To engineer clinical-grade digital shields that protect outpatients in the blind spots of recovery, driven by lived experience and a relentless commitment to data sovereignty and community reinvestment."

Enterprise-Grade Architecture

Designed strictly for clinical deployment. We track behavior, not just vocabulary.

The Threat Cache

Cortex-Zero autonomously normalizes and tags incoming texts from high-risk burner phones, silencing them before they hijack patient attention. It operates entirely in the background.

Zero-Tolerance Guardian

If a patient attempts to reply to a flagged threat, the Guardian service instantly locks the screen and forces a clinical intervention. We neutralize coded manipulation the exact millisecond it happens.

Vanguard Telemetry

Stop relying on subjective memory. Generate objective GPS heatmaps and behavioral risk logs for clinical review. All telemetry is processed directly on the device.

Zero-Cloud Privacy

Patient health data and behavioral logs never leave the physical phone. By eliminating cloud servers entirely, we remove third-party data liability for your clinic.
