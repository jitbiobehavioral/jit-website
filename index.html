The Aftercare Gap

Why current digital health tools fail in the moments that matter most.

Passive Logging is Too Late

Current digital health tools rely on passive journaling. By the time a clinician reviews a "high-risk" flag in a web dashboard, the critical 15-minute window for intervention has already closed.

Privacy Compromises Honesty

Patients hesitate to honestly record cravings when they know the data is uploaded to corporate servers. Without absolute, guaranteed privacy, the intervention tool is rendered useless.

Active, Localized Shielding

The JIT Cortex-Zero Protocol operates entirely on the user's device, providing instantaneous intervention without ever compromising privacy.

100% Zero-Cloud

All processing happens locally on the device via SQLite. No journal entry or location data ever leaves the user's phone. This guarantees absolute honesty and eliminates facility liability.
